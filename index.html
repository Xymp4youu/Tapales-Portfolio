About

Just a student who loves to build things.

I started out curious about games, and ended up building real apps and websites. Here's a little about me.

I got interested in technology back in high school, not because of books, but because I was curious about how to make game rewards unlimited in the games I played. That simple curiosity slowly became a real passion when we started learning HTML in our Computer subject. Then during our Grade 10 portfolio project, I realized that I wanted to create application, software systems, and websites in the future.

Now, I'm a 4th year BSIT student at College of St. Catherine, learning programming, UI design, and how to build projects that are useful and reliable. Outside of coding, I enjoy playing basketball, dancing, playing online games, and watching or talking about movies with my friends.

My goal is to finish my degree, become a Senior Developer someday, and continue improving my skills by solving different kinds of problems.
